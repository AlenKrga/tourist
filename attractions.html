Attractions

Howth Head

Howth Head is a stunning coastal area located in Dublin, Ireland.

It is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike, offering breathtaking views of the Irish Sea and the surrounding countryside.

Visitors can hike along the cliff trails, take a boat tour to see the local seals and seabirds, or enjoy a meal at one of the area's seafood restaurants.

With its rich history, stunning natural beauty, and wealth of activities, Howth Head is a must-visit for anyone in Dublin.

Howth Castle

Howth Castle is a stunning medieval castle located in the seaside town of Howth, Ireland.

With a history that dates back over 800 years, the castle has played an important role in Ireland's history and has been home to some of the country's most prominent families.

Today, visitors can tour the castle and its beautiful gardens, and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside and the Irish Sea.

Whether you're a history buff, a lover of beautiful architecture, or simply looking for a unique experience, a visit to Howth Castle is a must.
